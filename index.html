About

Program Overview: The Campaign is a unique, unscripted competition series that blends advertising, content creation, and influencer marketing. Brands team up with creators to develop viral marketing campaigns.

Value Proposition: By showcasing the creative process, The Campaign amplifies brand awareness and elevates diverse voices while unlocking opportunities for both brands and creators.

The Problem

  • Brands struggle with influencer integration including ROI, pricing, and creative control.
  • Creators struggle with long-form, narrative-driven content formats.

The Solution

The Campaign integrates brands in a meaningful way while utilizing creators to drive viewership. It blends competition with collaboration and strategy.

The Format

Show Structure: The Campaign is a digital, 30-minute, unscripted competition series. Eight social media influencers are paired with brands in various industries to create and run unique digital campaigns. The team with the most viral campaign wins.

Season Timeline/ Episode List: The first half of the season is all about the Art. Creators will develop a campaign in collaboration with their partner companies. They’ll seek the advice and services of other content creators, advertising and marketing gurus, talent agents, and celebrities to create content guaranteed to catch the attention of millions.

EP1. MEET AND GREET: The high-stakes competition starts with introductions from the competing companies and their influencer counterparts. The host explains the competition and rules while providing an outline for the next month, including guest lectures, benchmarks, and more. [WEEK ONE]

EP2. THE PURPOSE: The creators dive deep into their partner companies, learning the driving mission behind their brands. Initial conversations are had, and creative visions are challenged as the creators attempt to understand their founder partners and their company's ethos. Will the teams align with their partners' goals, or will they struggle to create something representing their team? The clock is ticking as they prepare to craft their campaign. [WEEKS TWO AND THREE]

EP3. BLUEPRINTS AND BREAKTHROUGHS: Creators continue creating campaigns with their partner companies. Both creators and their partner companies are lectured by representatives from a leading digital advertising agency on creating an unbeatable campaign, including best practices and things to avoid. Armed with insights, the teams begin to craft their campaign. They'll develop strategies unique to their team's brands while combating their diverse personalities clashing to channel their creative differences into a cohesive plan. The pressure mounts as each team races to develop a winning campaign blueprint. [WEEKS FOUR, FIVE AND SIX]

EP4. THE PROCESS: Viewers get exclusive access to the creativity, conflicts, and chaos behind the scenes as teams move from concept to execution. From late-night content shoots to near-meltdowns over creative direction, every moment counts. Some teams will rise to the occasion, while others will crack under the pressure. [WEEKS SEVEN, EIGHT, AND NINE]

EP5. LESSONS AND REFLECTIONS: With their campaigns completed, teams come together to reflect on their progress and partnerships. From triumphs to missteps, nothing is off the table. Industry experts from top digital advertising agencies weigh in and offer valuable insights. As the first half of the season concludes, one question looms: who is best positioned for the final showdown? [WEEK TEN]

The second half of the season is about Science. The teams must grow campaigns. They may consult with marketers, programmers, and hackers. At the end of the season, the team with the most viral campaign wins.

EP6. THE REACH, STRATEGIC TARGETING: The second half of the competition series starts with teams analyzing their campaign performance. With identical marketing budgets, they must decide how to allocate resources to maximize engagement. After meeting with experts from digital analytic companies on ways to maximize their reach, debates ensue over platform strategies, including platform strategies, boosting posts and more. It's a race to capture the audience's attention. [WEEKS ELEVEN AND TWELVE]

EP7. POWER PLAY, EXTERNAL REINFORCEMENTS: Desperate to gain an edge, teams enlist outside experts, including consultants, influencer friends, and analytics companies, to supercharge their campaigns. From hiring data scientists to analyze engagement patterns to collaborating with social media influencers and even "growth hackers," the competition becomes fierce, and every purchase, view, and follow counts. As the clock ticks down, some teams are ready for the final push, while unexpected setbacks push other teams to their limits. [WEEKS THIRTEEN AND FOURTEEN]

EP8. THE FINISH: With a week left to promote their campaigns, the teams dig deep and do everything they can to ensure they have the most viral campaign. Tensions run high as the leaderboard fluctuates in real time. As the deadline passes, teams are full of emotions. The competition concludes with a special dinner and gala for the creators and their respective teams, where the winner of The Campaign is announced. They're presented with the grand prize. The evening concludes with team interviews as they reflect on their journey, triumphs, and lessons learned. [WEEK FIFTEEN]

Competition Rules: Influencers and brands are paired to create digital marketing campaigns. Teams receive identical marketing budgets. Campaigns are judged on creativity, engagement, and measurable impact. The most viral campaign wins the competition.

Participants

Brands: Companies from various industries, selected for their innovative products, growth potential, and alignment with The Campaign’s mission.

Content Creators/Influencers: Influential social media creators skilled in audience engagement and digital strategy, chosen for their creativity, reach, and ability to authentically represent diverse voices.

For Companies

Sponsorship Packages: Tiered sponsorships offer varying levels of exposure, including product placement, digital content integration, and branding opportunities throughout the series. Exclusive sponsorships provide naming rights and prominent visibility.
